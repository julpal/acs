American Chemical Society

WELCOME!

ACS is a chemistry club at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor. We are a student chapter of the national American Chemical Society. Our goals are to bring together students who are interested in chemistry to learn from each other, explore opportunities in the field, have fun and spread our love of science to other members of the community. We achieve our goals through a variety of activities. To see what we have done in the past check out our past events. To join ACS email our president at acs-prez@umich.edu or come to one of our upcoming events.

Questions? Email acs-prez@umich.edu
