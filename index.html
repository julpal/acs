Student Members of the American Chemical Society

The ACS student member organization is both an educational and social organization founded on a common interest in chemistry and an appreciation of its applications. Our mission is to enhance interest in chemistry and the sciences with people throughout the community, facilitate networking between students and faculty, and share the excitement of chemistry with one another.
