About Us

Joining ACS allows you to:

  • Meet other students interested in chemistry
  • Get involved in science-related events in the community
  • Have the opportunity to attend lab tours and presentations by chemists
  • Eat liquid nitrogen ice cream!!

Have any ideas of what you want ACS to do? Join and help make it happen!

Everyone is welcome. You do not have to be a chemistry major to join.

Joining is easy! Send a message to our president at acs-prez@umich.edu
and ask to be added to our email list or attend any of our upcoming events.

Questions? Email acs-prez@umich.edu
